ChravelApp is a group travel planning app. Organize trips with your group using shared tools for chat, calendar, expenses, and an AI travel assistant.

Features

• Group chat — Real-time messaging with reactions for your travel group.
• Shared calendar — Add events and agenda items; everyone on the trip sees updates.
• Expense splitting — Track payments, balances, and who owes whom.
• AI travel concierge — Ask questions in text or voice and get suggestions for places, dining, and activities.
• Trip itineraries — Keep flights, lodging, and daily plans in one place.
• Invitations — Share a trip link to add travelers to the group.
• Push notifications — Get notified about chat messages, calendar changes, and expense requests.

Account sign-in supports email, Google, and Apple.

Subscriptions
ChravelApp offers optional subscription tiers (Explorer and Frequent Chraveler) that unlock additional features. Subscriptions auto-renew and can be managed in your device store account settings.

Privacy
